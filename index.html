Privacy Policy — Medsave

This Privacy Policy explains how Medsave collects, uses, and protects your information when using the Medsave.

1. Introduction

Our app provides reliable and structured health service information specifically designed for corporate employees and business organizations. It helps companies and their workforce stay informed about medical facilities, wellness programs, insurance benefits, and preventive health check-ups.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of data:

  • Personal identification data such as phone number and email address
  • Website-generated unique Employee Code and Policy Code

  • Medical or claim-related information provided for processing services

3. How We Use Your Information

Medsave uses collected data to:

  • Provide services within the MPPGCLMEDSAVE App
  • Verify identity and process claims or requests
  • Improve App performance and user experience
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Information may be shared only with:

  • Authorized service providers assisting in App and Website operations
  • Government authorities or regulators where legally mandated

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary for App services or legal compliance.

7. Your Rights
